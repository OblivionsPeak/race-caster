RaceCaster

The AI commentary booth for iRacing — live play-by-play spoken over your stream, generated from telemetry, running entirely on your PC.

⬇ Download RaceCaster.exe Source on GitHub
real output — 10 calls from the demo race, 60 seconds

What it calls

Overtakes & lead changes

Position swaps in the top 10, with special drama reserved for the lead.

Battles brewing

Sustained sub-0.8s gaps get called before the move happens — like a real spotter-caster.

Pit cycles

In-laps and out-laps for the front runners.

Fastest laps & flags

Purple laps, cautions, restarts, white flag, checkers and the podium.

Setup

  1. Clone or download the repo, run setup.bat once
  2. run-demo.bat — hear it commentate a simulated race, no sim needed
  3. run-live.bat with iRacing open — race or spectate, the booth calls it
  4. OBS: capture desktop audio for the voice; point a Text source at racecaster_caption.txt for captions

Who it's for

Solo streamers

Your race, with a commentary track. It calls your battles while you drive them.

League broadcasts

Run it on the spectator/stream PC — commentary every round, no volunteer burnout.
